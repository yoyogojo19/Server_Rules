# **Server Rules**

*Rules for the programming server to ensure a safe, productive, and inclusive environment.*

## **📜 Fundamental Rules**

*Not following these rules may result in consequences ranging from warnings to permanent bans, depending on the severity of the action.*

1. **Use common sense** while using this server. Respect others and the purpose of each channel.
2. **No NSFW or 18+ content** is allowed. Posting such content will result in an **immediate and permanent ban**.
3. The **`guides-...` channels** are for **information only**. If you want to discuss unrelated topics, use the appropriate channels.
4. **I am not a genius**: If I can’t answer your question, we can work together to find a solution, but I won’t do the work for you.
5. **Intimidation, harassment, or bullying** toward anyone will result in a **4-day ban**. Repeat offenses may lead to a permanent ban.

7. The **music recommendation channel** is for sharing music that is **LGBTQ+-friendly, non-racist, and free of unsensored swear words**.

## **🔧 Additional Rules (Suggestions)**

### **Code and Collaboration**

8. **No spam or flooding** in any channel. This includes repeated messages, excessive emojis, or irrelevant content.
9. **Share code responsibly**: If posting code snippets, ensure they are **properly formatted** (use code blocks with the correct language tag, e.g., python).
10. **No plagiarism**: Do not share or claim others' code/work as your own without credit.
11. **Avoid off-topic discussions** in technical channels. Use general or off-topic channels for non-programming conversations.

### **Security and Privacy**

12. **Do not share personal information** (e.g., addresses, phone numbers, passwords) in public channels.
13. **No malicious code**: Sharing viruses, malware, or harmful scripts is **strictly prohibited** and will result in an **immediate permanent ban**.
14. **Respect copyright**: Do not share pirated software, books, or other copyrighted materials.

### **Community and Behavior**

15. **No advertising** without permission. This includes promoting other servers, products, or services.
16. **Any language is accepte** we all have Google translate if don't understand, so discrimination is prohibited and will resulte in a **2-4 day ban**.
17. **No political or religious debates**.
18. **Avoid controversial topics** that may lead to arguments (e.g., sensitive social issues).

### **Moderation**

19. **Moderators' decisions are final**. Arguing with moderators in public channels may result in a warning or ban.
20. **Report issues**: If you see rule violations, report them to a moderator via DM or the designated reporting channel.

---

## **⚠️ Consequences**

- **First offense**: Warning or temporary mute (1-24 hours).
- **Repeat offenses**: Temporary ban (1-7 days).
- **Severe violations** (e.g., NSFW, harassment, malware): **Immediate permanent ban**.
